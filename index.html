Artelier Case Study

Designing an intelligent e-commerce experience that merges creativity, personalization, and technology.

01. Problem

Traditional e-commerce platforms lack personalization and emotional engagement, especially in creative industries like art. Users often feel disconnected from products and overwhelmed by choices.

02. Target Audience

Art enthusiasts, young creatives, and online shoppers seeking personalized, interactive, and culturally inclusive experiences.

03. Wireframes

Designed low-fidelity wireframes to map user journeys, focusing on intuitive navigation, product discovery, and smooth checkout flow.

04. Design Decisions

Implemented a clean and minimal UI with soft color palettes to highlight artwork. Integrated multilingual support and chatbot assistance to improve accessibility and user engagement.

05. Final Solution

Developed a fully functional platform with secure authentication, dynamic product listings, and a database-driven checkout system. Enhanced the experience with mood-based art generation and interactive AI features.

06. Reflection

This project strengthened my ability to merge design with backend logic, creating a product that is both visually engaging and technically robust. It also deepened my understanding of user-centered design in complex systems.
